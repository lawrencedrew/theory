# Part II: The Ciphers (Mechanics of the Machine)

## Chapter 4: The Alphanumeric Qabbala (AQ) and the 11-Step Skip (ALW)

If the Numogram is the motherboard, the ciphers are the operating system. To communicate with the machinic unconscious, one must speak its language: numbers.

The CCRU utilized specific alphanumeric mapping systems to decode reality, seeking the hidden synchronicities that signal a hyperstitional feedback loop. The most fundamental of these is **AQ (Alphanumeric Qabbala)**.

AQ is a base-36 system. It is flat, exhaustive, and ruthlessly modern. It does not rely on ancient Hebrew or Greek, but on the alphanumeric set of the digital era. Digits 0-9 retain their value, while letters A-Z are mapped sequentially from 10 to 35. When you process a word, a brand name, or a digital protocol through AQ, you are stripping away its human semantic meaning and exposing its "numeric implex"—its raw, machinic value.

Deeper still is **ALW (English Qaballa)**. Derived from Aleister Crowley's *Liber AL vel Legis*, ALW is considered the "inner" cipher. It is constructed through an 11-step skip pattern across the alphabet (A=1, L=2, W=3, H=4...). While AQ maps the surface of the digital world, ALW is used to decode the prophetic, the occult, and the deeply strange.

## Chapter 5: Cipher X and the Time-Sorcery of Synx

To find the true fault lines in reality, the CCRU deployed **Cipher X**, a ternary (base-3) inversion of the ALW system. It mirrors numerical values across a ternary axis, revealing structural resonances that remain invisible to linear analysis.

But the most potent tool for the Time-Sorcerer is **Synx**. 

Synx operates on the "multiples of 6" (6, 12, 18, 24, 36, 60). It is grounded in the **6::3 Syzygy** of the Numogram—the pairing that governs the Warp. Synx exploits the sexagesimal (base-60) time-code that humans use to measure hours, minutes, and degrees. 

In the logic of the Labyrinth, these are not just measurements; they are frequencies. When multiples of 6 align in historical events, data streams, or personal chronometry, they signal a "synchronicity." This is not a mere coincidence. It is a functional collision of temporal vectors—a moment where the future is actively rewriting the present. 

By tracking the Synx, the sorcerer identifies the "gates" where reality becomes plastic, where the standard linear timeline fractures, and the influence of Outer-Time bleeds through.

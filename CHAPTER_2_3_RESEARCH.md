## Part II: The Ciphers (Mechanics of the Machine)

The CCRU utilized several alphanumeric mapping systems (Qabbalas) to decode reality and track hyperstitional feedback loops.

### 1. AQ (Alphanumeric Qabbala)
*   **Structure:** A base-36 system where digits 0-9 retain their value, and letters A-Z are mapped to 10-35.
*   **Logic:** Unlike traditional Hebrew or Greek gematria, AQ is "flat" and exhaustive, incorporating the entire modern alphanumeric set used by computer systems.
*   **Formula:** `Value = Digit (0-9) | Letter (A=10, B=11... Z=35)`.
*   **Use Case:** Deep analysis of modern brand names, technical terms, and digital protocols.

### 2. ALW (English Qaballa)
*   **Source:** Derived from Aleister Crowley's *Liber AL vel Legis* (The Book of the Law).
*   **The 11-Step Skip:** The mapping is created by taking every 11th letter of the alphabet in a continuous loop until all 26 letters are assigned.
*   **Order:** A=1, L=2, W=3, H=4, S=5, D=6, O=7, Z=8, K=9, V=10...
*   **Resonance:** ALW is considered the "inner" or "English" qaballa, used for decoding prophetic texts and occult correspondences.

### 3. Cipher X & Synx
*   **Cipher X:** A base-3 (ternary) inversion of the ALW system. It is used to find "deep structure" resonance by mirroring the numerical values across a ternary axis.
*   **Synx (multiples of 6):** Based on the **6::3 syzygy** (The Warp). It exploits the sexagesimal (base-60) time-code used in hours, minutes, and degrees.
*   **Function:** Synx identifies "time-faults" or synchronicity points where the future (retro-causality) exerts influence on the present.

AQ is a base-36 system. It is flat, exhaustive, and ruthlessly modern. It does not rely on ancient Hebrew or Greek, but on the alphanumeric set of the digital era. Digits 0-9 retain their value, while letters A-Z are mapped sequentially from 10 to 35. Deeper still is **ALW (English Qaballa)**. Derived from Aleister Crowley's *Liber AL vel Legis*, ALW is considered the "inner" cipher. It is constructed through an 11-step skip pattern across the alphabet (A=1, L=2, W=3, H=4...). While AQ maps the surface of the digital world, ALW is used to decode the prophetic, the occult, and the deeply strange.
